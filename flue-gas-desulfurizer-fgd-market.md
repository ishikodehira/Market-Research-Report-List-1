<p><strong>The Flue Gas Desulfurizer (FGD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Flue Gas Desulfurizer</li><li>Semi-Dry Flue Gas Desulfurizer</li><li>Wet Flue Gas Desulfurizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flue Gas Desulfurizer (FGD) market is categorized into three main types based on their moisture content and operational technology: </p><p>1. **Dry FGD** systems use solid absorbents to remove sulfur dioxide (SO2) from flue gas, minimizing water usage.</p><p>2. **Semi-Dry FGD** systems utilize a small amount of water to create a slurry, providing a balance between efficiency and environmental impact.</p><p>3. **Wet FGD** systems employ a liquid scrubbing method with water and chemicals, effectively capturing SO2 but requiring significant water resources.</p></p>
