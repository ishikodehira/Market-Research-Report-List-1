<p><p>The 13X molecular sieve adsorbent market includes two main types: Molecular Sieve and Special Molecular Sieve. Molecular Sieves are crystalline aluminosilicates used for separating molecules based on size, effectively adsorbing smaller molecules and allowing larger ones to pass. Special Molecular Sieves are engineered for specific applications, enhancing performance in targeted adsorptive processes, such as gas separation, drying, or purification under unique conditions. Both types are vital in industries like petrochemicals, environmental protection, and pharmaceuticals for efficient molecular separation.</p></p>
