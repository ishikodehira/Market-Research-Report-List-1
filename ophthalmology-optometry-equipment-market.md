<p><p>The ophthalmology and optometry equipment market includes various devices essential for eye care. An OCT device captures cross-sectional images of the retina, while fundus cameras provide detailed images of the eye's interior. Slit lamps facilitate the examination of anterior eye structures, and tonometers measure intraocular pressure. Operating microscopes are used during surgeries, while autorefractors and keratometers assess refractive errors and corneal shape. Ophthalmoscopes and retinoscopes are utilized for examining the eye's fundus and refractive assessment, respectively. Other market types encompass additional specialized tools and technologies.</p></p>
