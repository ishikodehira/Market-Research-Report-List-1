<p><ul><li>Aluminum Core PCB</li><li>Cooper Core PCB</li><li>Alloys Core PCB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MCPCB market comprises three main types: Aluminum Core PCBs, Copper Core PCBs, and Alloy Core PCBs. Aluminum Core PCBs are widely used for their excellent thermal conductivity and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for LED applications. Copper Core PCBs offer superior thermal management and are utilized in high-power applications, despite being more expensive. Alloy Core PCBs combine the benefits of both materials, providing enhanced thermal performance and durability, suitable for specialized applications that require robust heat dissipation.</p></p>
