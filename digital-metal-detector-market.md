<p><strong>The Digital Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital metal detector market is divided into three main types: Very Low Frequency (VLF), Pulse Induction (PI), and Beat-Frequency Oscillation (BFO). VLF detectors use two coils to transmit and receive signals, offering high sensitivity for detecting small items. Pulse Induction detectors utilize short bursts of energy for deeper penetration and can handle mineralized soil effectively. Beat-Frequency Oscillation detectors generate two audio frequencies, allowing users to identify metal objects based on the difference in sound, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
