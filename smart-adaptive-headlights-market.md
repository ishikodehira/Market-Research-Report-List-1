<p><strong>The Smart Adaptive Headlights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AFS</li><li>ABD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart adaptive headlights enhance vehicle visibility by automatically adjusting light patterns based on driving conditions. The market is primarily divided into two types: Adaptive Front-lighting Systems (AFS) and Adaptive Beam Drivers (ABD). AFS uses sensors to alter the direction and intensity of headlights based on vehicle speed and steering angle, improving cornering visibility. Conversely, ABD focuses on adjusting the headlight beam's distribution and intensity dynamically, optimizing visibility for various situations, enhancing safety and driving comfort.</p></p>
