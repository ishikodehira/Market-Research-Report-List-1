<p><p>The Packaged MEMS Mirror Chips market is categorized into 1-axis and 2-axis scan mirrors, each serving distinct applications. 1-axis scan mirrors are primarily used for simple scanning tasks, typically in laser projectors and barcode scanners, allowing movement along a single plane. Conversely, 2-axis scan mirrors enable more complex operations by facilitating movement in two perpendicular axes, making them ideal for advanced applications like LIDAR systems, digital displays, and optical communication, enhancing versatility and functionality in various industries.</p></p>
