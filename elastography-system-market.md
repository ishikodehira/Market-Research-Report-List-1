<p><p>The elastography system market consists of several key types: Ultrasound Elastography, Magnetic Resonance Elastography (MRE), and others. Ultrasound elastography utilizes high-frequency sound waves to assess tissue stiffness, making it popular for liver and thyroid evaluations. Magnetic Resonance Elastography employs magnetic resonance imaging to visualize tissue elasticity, providing detailed insights into soft tissue disorders. Other types may include techniques like optical elastography or tactile imaging, each offering unique advantages for assessing tissue health and disease diagnosis across various medical fields.</p></p>
