<p><p>The Oxygen Conserving Device market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic and electronic devices. In contrast, electronic devices employ advanced technology, incorporating sensors and microprocessors to deliver precise oxygen doses, enhancing patient comfort and efficiency.
