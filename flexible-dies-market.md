<p><ul><li>Laser Hardened</li><li>Not Laser Hardened</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible dies market is categorized into two types: laser hardened and not laser hardened. Laser hardened dies are treated with laser technology, enhancing their durability and wear resistance, making them suitable for high-volume production and longer operational life. In contrast, not laser hardened dies are typically less robust, offering a cost-effective solution for lower volume applications or shorter lifespans. The choice between these types depends on production needs, budget constraints, and performance requirements in the packaging and printing industries.</p></p>
