<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump</li><li>Double Roller Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass machine market is segmented primarily into single roller pump and double roller pump types. Single roller pumps utilize a single mechanism to propel blood, offering simplicity and reduced risk of hemolysis but may have limitations in flow rates. Conversely, double roller pumps feature two rollers that provide higher flow and more consistent perfusion pressures, enhancing patient safety during complex surgeries. Both types are critical in facilitating heart-lung functions during cardiac procedures, catering to specific surgical requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
