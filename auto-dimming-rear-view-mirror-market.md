<p><p>The auto-dimming rearview mirror market is divided into two primary types: outer auto-dimming mirrors and inside auto-dimming mirrors. Outer auto-dimming mirrors are typically mounted on the vehicle's side and automatically adjust the tint based on external light, reducing glare from headlights of vehicles behind. Inside auto-dimming mirrors, found within the cabin, serve a similar purpose but focus on rearview visibility, enhancing safety by minimizing glare while driving at night or in low-light conditions.</p></p>
