<p><p>Benchtop TDS meters are essential tools for measuring the total dissolved solids (TDS) in liquids. The market is segmented into single-channel, dual-channel, and multi-channel types. Single-channel meters are designed for straightforward, individual measurements, making them user-friendly for basic applications. Dual-channel meters allow simultaneous testing of two samples, enhancing efficiency. Multi-channel meters can analyze multiple samples at once, ideal for larger laboratories or research settings. Each type caters to varying user needs and operational scales in water quality assessment.</p></p>
