<p><p>The electrode catheter market is divided mainly into two types: floating electrode catheters and common electrode catheters. Floating electrode catheters are designed to provide enhanced stability and improved signal quality during electrophysiological procedures, as they are capable of adapting to the movement of heart tissue. On the other hand, common electrode catheters are more standard in design, widely used for various diagnostic and therapeutic applications but may lack the specialized features of floating varieties. Both types serve crucial roles in cardiac interventions.</p></p>
