<p><ul><li>Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems</li><li>Fully Fly-By-Wire Controls Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft fly-by-wire system market comprises two primary types: Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems and Fully Fly-By-Wire Control Systems. Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems serve as a safety net, allowing pilots to control the aircraft through traditional manual systems in case of fly-by-wire failure. Conversely, Fully Fly-By-Wire Control Systems utilize electronic systems to manage flight controls, offering enhanced precision, reduced weight, and increased maneuverability, while improving safety through advanced computing and redundancy features.</p></p>
