<p><p>The consumer mobile payments market is divided into two main types: remote and proximity payments. Remote payments enable consumers to make purchases online or through apps without being physically present, utilizing technologies like digital wallets or payment links. In contrast, proximity payments occur when users transact in-store or at physical locations using their mobile devices, often employing NFC (Near Field Communication) or QR codes for instant transactions. Both types enhance convenience and speed in consumer spending.</p></p>
