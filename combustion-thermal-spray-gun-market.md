<p><p>The combustion thermal spray gun market encompasses various techniques such as flame spraying, HVOC (High Velocity Oxy-Fuel) spraying, and detonation flame spraying. Flame spraying utilizes a fuel-oxidizer mixture to melt and project coating materials, ideal for diverse surfaces. HVOC spraying enhances coating velocity and adhesion through high-temperature flames, offering superior coating properties. Detonation flame spraying employs controlled explosive combustion to achieve high-energy coatings, ensuring dense and uniform layers. Each method caters to specific industrial applications, emphasizing efficiency and durability in coating solutions.</p></p>
