<p><p>Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) refers to a comprehensive approach to cybersecurity that focuses on identifying and responding to threats on endpoint devices such as laptops, desktops, and servers. This proactive approach enhances incident response capabilities and helps mitigate the damage from cyber threats.</p><p>The Endpoint Detection and Response Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing sophistication of cyber threats and the rising adoption of remote working practices. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period, fueled by advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies that enhance threat detection and response capabilities. </p><p>Key trends include the integration of EDR with security information and event management (SIEM) systems, the growing emphasis on automated response mechanisms, and the shift towards cloud-based EDR solutions.
