<p><p>Telecom Cloud Billing Services encompass various market types. Embedded Billing integrates billing directly within applications or services, enhancing user experience. Convergent Billing consolidates multiple services like voice, data, and messaging into a single bill, simplifying customer management. Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Billing focuses on direct transactions between telecom providers and their end-users, improving revenue flow. Prepaid Billing allows customers to pay in advance for services, promoting financial control and reducing payment defaults. Each type addresses specific needs and enhances billing efficiency in telecommunications.</p></p>
