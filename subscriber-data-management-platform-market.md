<p><p>Subscriber Data Management Platforms (SDMPs) are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based SDMPs are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, ease of access, and reduced maintenance for users, making them suitable for organizations seeking flexibility. In contrast, on-premises SDMPs are installed locally within a company’s infrastructure, providing enhanced security and control over data but requiring more significant investment in hardware and IT resources. Both types serve critical roles in managing subscriber information effectively.</p></p>
