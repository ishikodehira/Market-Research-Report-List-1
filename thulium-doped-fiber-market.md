<p><p>The Thulium-Doped Fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Clad Fiber and Double Clad Fiber. Single Clad Fiber consists of a single core encased in a cladding, offering simplicity and effective dopant concentration for applications like lasers and amplifiers. In contrast, Double Clad Fiber features an inner core for light propagation and an outer cladding that enhances pump power absorption, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Both types cater to specific needs in telecommunications and industrial sectors.</p></p>
