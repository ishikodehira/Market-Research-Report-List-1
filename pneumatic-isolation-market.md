<p><strong>The Pneumatic Isolation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push Pneumatic Isolation</li><li>Pull Pneumatic Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic isolation market is primarily divided into two types: push pneumatic isolation and pull pneumatic isolation. Push pneumatic isolation utilizes air pressure to create a barrier, effectively preventing fluid movement in pipelines during maintenance or operational changes. In contrast, pull pneumatic isolation employs negative pressure to retract and block fluids, allowing for efficient system control. Both methods are critical for enhancing safety and reliability in industrial applications, ensuring minimal risk during maintenance and operational procedures.</p></p>
