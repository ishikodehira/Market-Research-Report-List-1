<p><p>The maritime fenders market comprises three main types: solid rubber fenders, pneumatic fenders, and foam fenders. Solid rubber fenders are durable and provide high energy absorption, suitable for permanent docking facilities. Pneumatic fenders utilize air-filled chambers, offering excellent shock absorption and flexibility, ideal for larger vessels. Foam fenders are lightweight and resistant to water absorption, making them effective in various marine environments. Each type serves distinct applications, ensuring safe vessel operations and protecting infrastructure from impact.</p></p>
