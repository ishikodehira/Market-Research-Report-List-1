<p><p>The Sodium Chloride Ophthalmic Products market includes two primary types: Hypertonic Sodium Chloride Eye Ointment and Hypertonic Sodium Chloride Eye Drops. Hypertonic eye ointments are designed to reduce corneal edema by drawing excess fluid from the eye, offering prolonged moisture and relief. In contrast, hypertonic eye drops serve a similar purpose but provide a more convenient, quick-acting solution. Both formulations are essential for managing conditions like glaucoma and other disorders requiring fluid balance in the ocular environment.</p></p>
