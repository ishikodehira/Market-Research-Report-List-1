<p><p>Locknuts are specialized fasteners designed to secure assemblies by preventing loosening under vibrations and dynamic loads. There are primarily two market types: Prevailing Torque Locknuts, which create resistance through friction when tightened, ensuring a secure fit, and Surface-Bearing Locknuts, which use a flat bearing surface to distribute load and enhance stability. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including automotive and aerospace, ensuring reliability and safety in critical mechanical applications. The choice depends on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
