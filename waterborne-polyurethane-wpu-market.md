<p><p>Waterborne Polyurethane (WPU) is a type of polyurethane that is dispersed in water, making it a more environmentally friendly alternative to traditional solvent-based polyurethanes. The versatility, durability, and flexibility of WPU make it suitable for industries like automotive, construction, and textiles.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates a significant upward trend for the WPU market, driven by increasing demand for eco-friendly products and stringent environmental regulations.
