<p><strong>The Telecentric Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Object Square Telephoto Lens</li><li>Bi-Telecentric Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telecentric lenses market includes distinct types such as object square telephoto lenses and bi-telecentric lenses. Object square telephoto lenses are designed to maintain a constant magnification over varying object distances while keeping the image square and distortion-free. Bi-telecentric lenses feature dual telecentric designs for both object and image spaces, ensuring that parallel rays of light enter the lens, which enhances measurement accuracy and reduces distortions. These lenses are vital for precision applications in imaging and optical systems.</p></p>
