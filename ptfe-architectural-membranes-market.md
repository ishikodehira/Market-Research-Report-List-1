<p><strong>The PTFE Architectural Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE architectural membranes market features two primary types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic PTFE membranes. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes repel water, making them ideal for applications that require water resistance and durability, such as roofing and facades. In contrast, hydrophilic PTFE membranes absorb moisture, allowing for enhanced air permeability and breathability, suitable for applications like tents and outdoor structures. Both types offer unique benefits, catering to various architectural needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
