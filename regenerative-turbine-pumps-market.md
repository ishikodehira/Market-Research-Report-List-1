<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Regenerative Turbine Pumps</li><li>Multi-Stage Regenerative Turbine Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regenerative turbine pumps are specialized centrifugal pumps designed to handle low flow rates at high pressures. The single-stage regenerative turbine pump consists of one impeller, making it suitable for applications with moderate pressure requirements. In contrast, multi-stage regenerative turbine pumps feature multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure generation and greater versatility in demanding applications. Both types are used across various industries, including water supply, chemical processing, and agriculture, due to their efficiency and reliability in handling fluids.</p></p>
