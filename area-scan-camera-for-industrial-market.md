<p><p>Area scan cameras are widely used in the industrial market for applications such as quality control, automation, and robotics. They capture images of an entire scene in a single exposure, making them ideal for high-speed manufacturing processes. The two primary sensor types in this market are CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS cameras are typically faster, consume less power, and are more cost-effective, while CCD cameras offer superior image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for high-precision tasks.</p></p>
