<p><p>Fiber optic cable blowers are specialized machines used for installing fiber optic cables within duct systems. The market can be categorized into three main types: hydraulically operated, pneumatically operated, and electrically powered blowers. Hydraulically driven blowers utilize high-pressure hydraulic systems to propel cables, offering efficient installation in various terrains. Pneumatic blowers use compressed air to achieve similar results and are valued for their portability. Electric blowers, powered by electric motors, provide consistent performance and are suitable for indoor applications with limited space.</p></p>
