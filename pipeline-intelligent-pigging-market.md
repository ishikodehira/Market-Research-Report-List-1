<p><p>The Pipeline Intelligent Pigging market encompasses various types of inspection technologies, including Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL), Ultrasonic, and Caliper methods. MFL utilizes magnetic fields to detect corrosion and wall loss by analyzing the flux changes. Ultrasonic pigging employs sound waves to assess material thickness and detect flaws with high precision. Caliper pigs measure the internal geometry of pipelines, identifying deformations or blockages. Together, these technologies enhance pipeline integrity management and ensure safe operation in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
