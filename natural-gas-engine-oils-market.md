<p><p>The natural gas engine oils market primarily includes two types: ashless gas engine oil and low ash gas engine oil. Ashless gas engine oils are formulated without metallic additives, reducing deposits and contributing to cleaner engine operation, making them ideal for high-performance applications. On the other hand, low ash gas engine oils contain minimal levels of additives that form ash upon combustion, providing efficient protection while controlling emissions. Both types enhance engine longevity and performance in natural gas applications.</p></p>
