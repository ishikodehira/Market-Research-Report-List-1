<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Diagnostic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li><li>Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic diagnostic equipment market is divided into two primary types: continuous wave ultrasound equipment and pulsed wave ultrasound equipment. Continuous wave ultrasound uses a constant signal for real-time imaging, allowing for the observation of moving structures, making it ideal for cardiac evaluations. In contrast, pulsed wave ultrasound emits short bursts of sound waves, enabling depth determination and better resolution for stationary or slow-moving tissues, commonly used in obstetrics and gynecology. Both types play vital roles in medical diagnostics.</p></p>
