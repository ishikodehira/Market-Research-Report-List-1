<p><p>The Influenza Drugs and Vaccines market is divided into two main types: Influenza Drugs and Influenza Vaccines. Influenza Drugs include antiviral medications that treat or prevent influenza infections, such as neuraminidase inhibitors and polymerase inhibitors, aimed at reducing symptom severity and duration. Influenza Vaccines, on the other hand, are biological preparations designed to provide immunity against influenza viruses, primarily administered seasonally to prevent infection and mitigate outbreaks, optimizing public health and reducing healthcare costs associated with flu-related illnesses.</p></p>
