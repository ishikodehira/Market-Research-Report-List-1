<p><strong>The Video Camera Gimbal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Level Gimbal</li><li>All-round Gimbal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video camera gimbal market is primarily divided into two types: level gimbals and all-round gimbals. Level gimbals are designed to stabilize cameras primarily on a horizontal plane, making them suitable for static shots or limited movement. In contrast, all-round gimbals offer 3-axis stabilization, allowing for smooth filming even during dynamic movements, such as running or moving around obstacles. These gimbals cater to different filming styles, enhancing video quality across varied shooting conditions.</p></p>
