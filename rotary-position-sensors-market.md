<p><strong>The Rotary Position Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Rotary Position Sensors</li><li>Non-Contact Rotary Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary position sensors market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact rotary position sensors rely on physical contact between moving parts to detect position changes, offering high precision but subject to wear and tear. In contrast, non-contact rotary position sensors utilize magnetic, optical, or capacitive methods to measure rotational position without physical contact, providing longer lifespan and reduced maintenance. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, robotics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
