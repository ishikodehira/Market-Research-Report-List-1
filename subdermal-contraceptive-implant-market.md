<p><p>The Subdermal Contraceptive Implant is a long-acting reversible contraceptive method that involves the insertion of a small rod under the skin, typically in the upper arm. This implant releases hormones, primarily progestin, to prevent ovulation and ensure effective pregnancy prevention for up to three to five years, depending on the product. The advantages of this method include its convenience, high efficacy, and the absence of daily compliance, making it a preferred choice for many women seeking contraception.</p><p>The Subdermal Contraceptive Implant Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period.
