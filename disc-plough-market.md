<p><p>The disc plough market is categorized into three main types: trail-type, mounted type, and semi-mounted type. </p><p>The trail-type disc plough is towed by a tractor, ideal for larger fields due to its ability to cover more ground. The mounted type attaches directly to the tractor, offering enhanced maneuverability and ease of operation in smaller areas. The semi-mounted type combines features of both, providing stability and improved depth control while still being towed, making it versatile for varied terrains. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs and land conditions.</p></p>
